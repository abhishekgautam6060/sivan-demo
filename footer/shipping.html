Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1–3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or public holidays.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your location. We may offer free shipping on selected orders or promotional offers.

3. Delivery Time

Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days across India. Remote areas may require additional time depending on courier availability.

4. International Shipping

Currently, we offer limited international shipping. Delivery timelines and charges vary depending on the destination country.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS to monitor your shipment status.

6. Delays

Delivery delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather, courier issues, or high demand periods. We appreciate your patience.

7. Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package is damaged or lost during transit, please contact us immediately. We will assist you in resolving the issue as quickly as possible.
